Our Transport Network

U

Metro (U-Bahn)

Fast and efficient underground trains connecting all major districts of Vienna.

  • 5 lines (U1-U6)
  • 24/7 service on weekends
  • 2-5 minute intervals during peak hours
T

Tram

Extensive tram network providing convenient access throughout the city.

  • Over 30 lines
  • Scenic routes through the city
  • Low-floor vehicles for accessibility
B

Bus

Comprehensive bus network covering all areas of Vienna.

  • Day and night services
  • Real-time information
  • Bike transport available
N

NightBus

Reliable night service when other transport options are limited.

  • Operates 12:30 AM - 5:00 AM
  • Frequent departures
  • Safe and monitored

Sustainability & Innovation

Vienna's public transport system is a global leader in sustainability and innovation:

  • 100% of our electricity comes from renewable sources
  • Modern, energy-efficient vehicles across our network
  • Continuous expansion of bike-sharing and pedestrian zones
  • Smart city integration for seamless mobility

Accessibility

Vienna's public transport is designed to be accessible to everyone:

  • Step-free access at all metro stations
  • Audio and visual announcements on all vehicles
  • Priority seating for passengers with disabilities
  • Tactile guidance systems for visually impaired passengers
  • Assistance services available upon request
